What Stays & What Goes

Field work is completely unchanged. Only office-side manual work gets replaced by the app.

✅ Stays the Same

📝
Parchi (Physical Slip) — created on field every 24 hrs. Operators need tangible proof
📄
1st Copy to Operator — trust & transparency. Proof of work
✅
Authorized Person Checks — human verification on ground
💵
Daily Allowance Payment — continues as-is, just tracked in app
🔄
Manager Alternating Schedule — Mon/Wed/Fri & Tue/Thu/Sat rotation

❌ Gets Eliminated

📒
Physical Register Book — replaced by app. No more Sunil/Omprakash manual register
📋
2nd Copy to Register Staff — no register = no need for 2nd copy routing
💻
Manual Excel Data Entry — app IS the database. No separate Excel
📇
Paper Hajri Cards — digital attendance auto-built from parchi entries
📅
Month-end Hajri Collection & Cross-check — already in system, no collection
🧮
Manual Salary & Billing Sheets — auto-generated from app data
