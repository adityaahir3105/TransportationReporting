📱 Key Change: Manager Enters Data on Field

The same Hussain Bhai / Suresh Bhai who creates the parchi opens the mobile app and enters the data right there. No need to send 2nd copy to register staff. No need for office staff to re-enter in Excel. One entry at source — done.

Who Does What

Only 2 roles actively interact with the app. Everything else is automatic.

👷
Field Manager
On Field + Mobile App
  • Creates physical parchi (same as before)
  • Opens mobile app, enters parchi data
  • Takes 30 seconds per parchi
  • Simple form — dropdowns + numbers
  • Works offline, syncs when online
💼
Office Staff / Owner
Office + Web/Mobile App
  • Reviews & approves entered parchis
  • Approves allowance payments
  • Adds deductions at month-end
  • Generates & exports salary/billing
  • Views dashboard & all reports
🤖
System (Auto)
Automatic
  • Auto-records attendance from parchi
  • Auto-calculates daily allowance
  • Auto-generates salary sheet
  • Auto-generates billing (TON + HR)
  • Auto-generates all 8 reports
